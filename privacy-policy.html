Privacy Policy

Welcome to Reeden! This Privacy Policy (the "Policy") is designed to inform you about how we collect, use, store, and share your personal information, and the rights you have with respect to it.

1. Information Collection

We collect information including, but not limited to:

  • Usage data, such as how you use our services, pages you visit, links you click, etc.

2. Information Use

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Personalize your experience

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With your consent

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you may have the following rights:

  • Delete your personal information

  • Data portability

We use cookies and similar technologies to collect and store information about your use of our services. You can control cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect the availability of certain features.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we may have collected personal information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Policy from time to time. The updated Policy will be effective immediately upon posting on the website. We encourage you to review this Policy periodically to understand how we protect your information.
